My Work for UWB Student Life

I designed and developed an online form management system for University of Washington, Bothell, Student Life. The system can only be accessed by students, faculty, or staff of University of Washington.

Technologies used:

  • PHP with Zend Framework
  • JavaScript with Dojo Toolkit
  • MySQL database
  • XHTML 1.0 and CSS2
  • Adobe Photoshop

Key highlights:

  • Creation and management of custom online forms.
  • Integrated submission system for custom forms.
  • Collaborator management to enable groups to manage a form lifecycle.
  • Integrated decision management system to facilitate allocating grant funds and/or reviewing submissions.
  • Public and private commenting system.
  • Ability to export all or some fields of submissions to spreadsheets.
