Sunny for JavaScript

You give it a time and a location, it'll give you the sunset and sunrise information (along with more specific sun-position-related data).

For example: var time = {year:1987, month:12, day: 22}; var space = {latitude: 47.60621, longitude: -122.332071}; // Istanbul, Turkey var sunrise = Sunny.sunrise(space, time); alert("The sun began to rise over Istanbul, Turkey at " + sunrise.getHours() + ":" + sunrise.getMinutes() + " on 12/22/1987.");

Sunny just does math to come up with the information (courtesy of the public domain solar calculator by NOAA). The calculation should provide accurate results from 2000 BCE to 3000 CE.
